👤 Anonymization

Employee names are replaced with anonymized IDs before being processed by our model. The mapping between IDs and names is stored separately with access restricted to authorized HR personnel only.

📝 Consent Tracking

All predictions are tied to explicit consent records. Employees can withdraw consent at any time, which immediately stops all future predictions and triggers data handling review.

Employee Rights

  • ✓ Right to Explanation - Every employee has the right to receive a plain-language explanation of any prediction made about them.
  • ✓ Right to Access - Employees can request to see all data used in their predictions at any time.
  • ✓ Right to Deletion - Under GDPR, employees can request deletion of their prediction history.
  • ✓ Right to Opt-Out - Employees can opt-out of turnover predictions without affecting their employment status.
  • ✓ Right to Human Review - All high-risk predictions require manual HR review before any action.
